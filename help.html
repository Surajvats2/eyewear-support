Frequently asked questions aur support

🛍️ Orders & Payments

Order kaise karta hai? ▼
Index page pe glasses browse karo → Cart mein add karo → Checkout pe jao → UPI se pay karo → UTR number enter karo. Order confirm ho jaata hai.
Konse payment methods accept hote hain? ▼
Hum UPI accept karte hain — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, ya koi bhi UPI app. Payment ke baad UTR/Transaction ID enter karni hogi.
Invoice kahan milegi? ▼
Payment confirm hone ke turant baad invoice page automatically open ho jaata hai. Wahan se PDF download ya print kar sakte ho.

🔧 Repair & Appointments

Glasses repair kaise book karein? ▼
"Fix Your Glasses" page pe jao → Toot e hue glasses ki photo upload karo → AI detect karega problem → Date aur time choose karo → Appointment confirm ho jaati hai.
Repair kitne time mein hoti hai? ▼
Simple repairs (screw, nose pad) 1-2 din mein. Frame replacement ya lens repair 3-5 working days le sakti hai.

📦 Delivery & Returns

Delivery kitne din mein aati hai? ▼
Jehanabad aur nearby areas mein 2-3 din. Bihar ke baaki areas mein 4-6 din. All India delivery available hai.
Return policy kya hai? ▼
7 din return policy hai. Product unopened aur original packaging mein hona chahiye. Damaged products ke liye full refund milega.

Aur help chahiye?

Directly humse contact karo — hum 24 ghante ke andar reply karte hain
