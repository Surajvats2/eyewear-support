Our Story

SpecAI started with a simple belief — great eyewear shouldn't cost a fortune. Founded in Jehanabad, Bihar, we combine precision craftsmanship with modern technology to bring you frames that are both beautiful and functional.

Every pair of glasses we sell is carefully selected for quality, comfort, and style. Our AI-powered repair booking system helps customers get their beloved frames fixed quickly and conveniently.
