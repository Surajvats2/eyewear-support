Glossary of Terms

Eyewear aur optics ke sabhi important terms explained in simple language

ABCLOPUV

A

Acetate

Ek high-quality plastic material jo eyeglass frames banane mein use hota hai. Ye lightweight, durable aur vibrant colors mein available hota hai. Italian acetate sabse premium maana jaata hai.

Anti-Reflective Coating (AR)

Lens pe ek special coating jo reflection aur glare ko reduce karti hai. Screen dekhte waqt, driving karte waqt aur raat ko bohot helpful hai.

Aspheric Lens

Traditional spherical lenses se flat design mein alag — yeh thinner, lighter hoti hain aur distortion kam hota hai, especially high prescriptions mein.

B

Blue Light Blocking (BLB)

Lens technology jo screens (mobile, laptop, TV) se nikalne wali harmful blue light ko filter karti hai. Aankhon ki thakaan aur neend ki problem mein help karta hai.

Bridge

Eyeglasses ka woh part jo dono lenses ko naak ke upar connect karta hai. Perfect fit ke liye bridge size zaroori hai.

C

Cat-eye Frame

Ek classic frame style jisme corners upar ki taraf uthe hue hote hain — vintage glamour look deta hai. Oval aur round chehere pe best lagta hai.

CR-39 Lens

Sabse common plastic lens material — lightweight, scratch-resistant aur affordable. Standard prescriptions ke liye ideal.

Crystal-Clear Lens

Completely transparent lens without any tint. Indoor use ke liye best — maximum clarity provide karta hai.

L

Lens Index

Lens ki thickness measure karne ka system. Higher index = thinner lens. 1.50 standard, 1.67 thin, 1.74 ultra-thin hota hai. High power mein high index recommend hota hai.

O

Optical Frame

Prescription lenses hold karne wala frame — yeh sunglass frame se alag hota hai kyunki isme clear lenses lagti hain vision correction ke liye.

P

Polarized Lens

Special lens jo horizontal glare (paani, road, snow se aane wali) ko block karti hai. Driving aur outdoor activities ke liye ideal.

Progressive Lens

Teen vision zones ek hi lens mein — distance, intermediate aur near. Bifocal ka modern upgrade, bina visible line ke.

U

UV Protection

Lens coating jo sun ki UV-A aur UV-B rays se aankhon ko protect karti hai. Long-term eye health ke liye zaroori hai, especially Indian climate mein.

UTR Number

Unique Transaction Reference — UPI payment ke baad milne wala transaction ID. SpecAI pe order confirm karne ke liye yeh enter karna hota hai.

V

Visual Acuity

Aankhon ki dekhne ki taakat ka measure. 6/6 (ya 20/20) perfect vision hai. Eye test mein check hota hai.
